Spirograph

The spirograph equation for three or more wheels can be generalized as follows:

$$z(t) = \sum_{k=1}^n a_k e^{i2\pi(n_kt+\theta_k)}$$

This program solves those equations for three wheels, assuming all of the $a$ coefficients are 1 and $\theta$ coefficients are 0. Find more details online at
http://linuxgazette.net/133/luana.html
